Goodbye from the FurMorphed Forums

The FurMorphed forums ran for more than 10 years and in that time the internet changed a great deal. The forum platform changed from the favoured method for communication, as it was back in 2007, to just a memory. Activity in the FurMorphed community is still supported via Discord and through Twitter but the forum itself finds very little place in the modern and mobile faster paced instant internet.

Although large forums do still exist FurMorphed was never large, it was close and it was friendly, but the level of use the forums see no longer warrants supporting them over maintaining the community on other promising platforms.

The forums are gone but FurMorphed is not...

I have enjoyed every moment of managing FurMorphed since founding it and I hope to continue for many years into the future on other platforms.

For me nothing will compare to the pride I felt seeing the forum so busy, so friendly and happy to help everyone and anyone. Thank you all for making it happen.

~ Kan Thorne - FurMorphed Admin 2007 - 2018
