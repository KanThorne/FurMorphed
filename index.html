The Forums are no longer with us...

The FurMorphed Forums ran for more than 10 years and the internet changed a great deal in that time. The forum platform fell from the favoured method for communication, as it was back in 2007, to almost a memory. Activity in the FurMorphed community is still supported via Discord and through Twitter but the forum itself found very little place in the modern and mobile faster paced internet.

Although large forums do still exist FurMorphed was never large just friendly and for the type of traffic the forums saw it no longer warranted supporting them over maintaining the community on other promising platforms.

...but FurMorphed is still around!

I have enjoyed every moment of managing FurMorphed since founding it and I hope to continue for many years into the future on other platforms.

For me nothing will compare to the pride I felt seeing the forum so busy, so friendly and happy to help everyone and anyone. Thank you all for making it happen.

~ Kan Thorne - FurMorphed Admin 2007 - 2018
